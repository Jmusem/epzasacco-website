Borrow Wisely

In order to access any of the sacco's products a member must satisfy the following conditions

  • Save for at least 6 months.
  • Total deductions ≤ 2/3 of gross salary.
  • Loans must be guaranteed by other members’ deposits.

Background

EPZA SACCO Ltd stands for EPZA Savings and Credit Cooperative Society Limited and was started in 1995. The key objective of the SACCO is to mobilize savings from members and advance credit to members from their savings.

About EPZA SACCO

EPZA SACCO is formed by members who are employees from Export Processing Zones Authority (EPZA) and constituent EPZA Enterprises within the country. Currently EPZA SACCO has a membership of about 2500. Members who exit from the EPZ fraternity may remain members of EPZA SACCO, if they wish so, after making special arrangements with the Central Management Committee.

How to Become a Member

To join, fill out a membership form and submit it to the EPZA SACCO office along with the following:

  • Copy of National ID
  • Two passport size photos
  • KRA PIN Certificate
  • Ksh 500 one-time registration fee
  • Minimum Ksh 1000 monthly savings via check-off

Services We Offer

Join us and make your money work for you

Development and Emergency Loans

DEVELOPMENT LOANS

Members qualify for 3 times of their cash deposits and the maximum repayment period is 48 months.

EMERGENCY LOANS

Emergency loan is awarded based on 1/3 rule and 3 times the net deposits. The maximum repayment period is 12 months.

School Fees and Special/Product Loans

SCHOOL FEES LOANS

School fees loan is awarded based on 1/3 rule and 3 times the net deposits. The maximum repayment period is 12 months.

SPECIAL/PRODUCT LOANS

Special/Product loan is a type of loan offered as physical product. It is offered subject to 1/3 rule and is payable within six months at an interest rate of 2% per month.

Top-up Loans and Christmas Fund

TOP-UP LOANS

This loan product is available to members who have an active loan product and would like to borrow the same product. It is applicable on development, emergency and school fees loan products. The loan amount and repayment period (subject to a maximum of 48 months) depends on the amount required and the total loans owed to the SACCO.

CHRISTMAS FUND

This is a facility to assist SACCO members to save for their Christmas festivities. A member saves any amount of money with the SACCO on monthly basis which is paid back in December just before businesses close for Christmas and New Year Holidays.

Asset Finance and Masaa Loan

ASSET FINANCE

Members qualify for 4 times of their cash deposits and maximum repayment period is 60 months at 14% interest.

MASAA LOANS

Masaa loan is applicable to salaried members whose salaries are processed by Export Processing Zones Authority and Constituent EPZ Companies. It is recovered once within a month and limited to net salary pay. Interest is charged on 10%.
